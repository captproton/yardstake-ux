Channel Partner Persona: Referring Rachel (Oregon Realtor / ADU Professional)

From Client Conversation to Closed Deal

Rachel works with Oregon homeowners every day. She doesn't build ADUs — but she knows who does, and she knows that a confident feasibility answer closes more deals. Yardstake becomes her secret weapon.

Variant A

Realtor Rachel

Oregon real estate agent. Sends buyer clients to Yardstake before listing consultation. ADU potential increases perceived home value and listing price.

Variant B

Lender Rachel

Credit union or RenoFi-type ADU lender. Refers pre-qualified borrowers who already have a $49 report — the report proves feasibility and speeds underwriting.

Variant C

Consultant Rachel

ADU permitting consultant or housing advocate. Sends every new client to the free check first — reduces their own intake burden and pre-qualifies project candidates.

Victor's Introduction

Victor calls Rachel — not a cold pitch. He tells her: "I have a tool that tells your clients in 60 seconds whether their lot qualifies for an ADU. It's free for them. It makes you look like the most informed agent in Portland." He sends her a personal referral link: yardstake.com/check?ref=rachel. Rachel runs the check on her own home address before she even hangs up.

Sticky by Design

Rachel doesn't churn. Her referral link is in her email signature, her client intake packet, and her listing consultation slide deck. She mentions Yardstake at every ADU conversation without being asked. When Yardstake launches in California, Rachel asks Victor if her Bay Area network counts. The Rachel relationship compounds over time with zero marginal cost.
