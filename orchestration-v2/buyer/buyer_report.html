Oregon ADU Financing Options

HELOC

Home Equity Line of Credit

Best option if you have equity in your home. Most Portland homeowners with 20%+ equity qualify. Current rates: 7–9%. Flexible draws — borrow what you need, when you need it.

Cash-Out Refi

Refinance & Unlock Equity

Replace your mortgage at a higher balance. Best if your current rate is already above market. 30-year term available. Works well for all-in financing of a larger project.

OHCS

Oregon ADU Loan Program

Oregon Housing & Community Services offers ADU-specific financing. Income limits apply. Below-market rates available. Contact OHCS at ohcs.oregon.gov for current terms.
